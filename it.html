Amazon is an American multinational technology company based in Seattle that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming and artificial intelligence. It is considered one of the big five tech companies that make up the FAANG acronym (Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix and Google). Amazon is the largest internet company by revenue in the world. It is the second largest employer in the United States and of the world's most valuable companies. The Amazon pay scale is good and above average as compared to other companies, it comes with several benefits and also gives you the freedom to move around.

With it's headquarters based roughly 15 miles outside of Seattle's downtown hub, it's no wonder Seattle is big in IT. Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen on the 4th of April in 1975. It soon rose to dominate the personal computer operating system market with it's release of the MS-DOS in the mid-1980s. They are also well known for its hardware products such as the Microsoft Surface and the Xbox video game consoles. Microsoft offers a wide range of world-class benefits designed to help you and your family live life well. We offer competitive pay, bonuses and stock awards to eligible employees based on individual performance, as well as benefits to help you lead a healthy life, save for the future and enjoy your journey here at Microsoft.

SAP Concur is an American (Software as a Service or "SaaS") company providing travel and expense management services to businesses. It is headquartered roughly 10 miles away from Seattle in beatiful Bellevue, Washington. In September they acquired Concur Technologies and added Concur to their name (SAP SE was their name before the "merge") and the deal was completed in December of 2014. Their main competitors are Expedia Group, Trivago and TripAdvisor. Concur is a great place to work at because they've been awarded the best place to work at for four years in total.

Tableau Software is an American interactive data visualization software company founded in January of 2003 by Christian Chabot, Pat Hanrahan and Chris Stolte, in Mountain View, California. The company is currently headquartered in Seattle, Washington. Tableau focuses on business intelligence. Tableau has mapping software that is able to plot latitude and longitude coordinates and connect to spatial files that can display custom geography
