While we outsiders may call Seattle, "Seattle", it's residents prefer to call it The City of Flowers (est. 1942) and/or The Emerald City (est. 1981 via a contest held that allowed its residents to vote on the name) as these are the names that the beautiful city of Seattle has been donned. With over 750,000+ residents, Seattle is home to all walks of life including but not limited to; Native Americans, Scandinavians, Asian Americans, African Americans as well as a large thriving LGBTQ community ranking at the 6th largest in the nation.
